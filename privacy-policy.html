Privacy Policy - Laughing Albatross

Last updated: May 28, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Laughing Albatross ("we", "our", or "us") handles information in connection with our application "Oiwai SNS Auto Post" (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

Our App is designed to automatically post pre-produced birthday greeting videos to social media platforms. We collect and process the following information:

  • TikTok Account Information: When you authorize our App, we access your TikTok account to post content on your behalf. We only request permissions necessary for content posting.
  • Usage Data: We may collect basic usage logs (upload timestamps, success/failure status) for operational purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Posting pre-produced birthday greeting videos to your TikTok account
  • Monitoring upload success and troubleshooting errors
  • Improving the reliability of our automated posting service

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • As required by law or legal process
  • With TikTok, as necessary to provide the posting functionality

5. Data Retention

We retain OAuth tokens and basic usage logs only as long as necessary to provide our services. You may revoke access at any time through your TikTok account settings.

6. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including encrypted storage of authentication tokens.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
